Scan to Login

Quick and secure login
A video demonstrating how to login securely by scanning QR code or phone login.

Bind account

1. Open WhatsApp on your phone, click the link with phone number, and enter this code on your phone!

Login to WhatsApp
  1. Open WhatsApp
  2. Tap Settings ⚙️ (on iPhone) or Menu ☰ (on Android)
  3. Tap Linked Devices then Link a Device
  4. Point your phone at this screen and scan the QR code
  5. Switch to phone number association, enter verification code 77777777
  6. While the conversation is loading, keep both apps open and connected to the network (Wi-Fi, 4G or faster)
